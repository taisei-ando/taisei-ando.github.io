I have enrolled as a PhD student in the **Graduate School of Information Sciences, Tohoku University**, as a member of the [Tough Robotics Lab](https://tr.is.tohoku.ac.jp/).

My research will focus on Physical AI, SLAM, and Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC), building on my master's work to develop robust localization and mapping systems that combine robotics and wireless sensing.
